About us

We are aiming to create a tight-knit exclusive community where every member can provide ideas and inputs and make sure their voices are heard. We are inspired by other community projects like Godjira, Llamaverse, Zooverse, AIC, etc, and we are looking for like minded people, who believes in the power of the communities in the NFT space and wanna help us building something we can call ours!!
So far we plan to introduce an entire ecosystem with new NFT experiences and utilities, including some P2E mechanics, risk based games and even an NFT tournament system, yet we want our community to make the guidelines of what and when are we releasing our utilities and products.

FAQs

What is The Royal Phoenix collection size?

There will be only 300 Genesis Royal Phoenix.

How many attributes will be in the collection?

Genesis collection will be automatically generated using 100+ different hand drawn attributes that will include: backgrounds, skins, hats, accesories, eyes, mouth, clothes and more.

What will be the mint price?

TBD - Expect a low mint price. Approx .05-.07ETH.

What are the mint dates?

TBD - This will be determined by the community.

What are the utilities of The Royal Phoenix?

We want this to be a community guided project, yet we have planned to create an ecosytem. Feel free to check our current plans on our Discord or Whitepaper.

How many Royal Phoenix NFTs can I mint?

Only 1 per wallet.

Do Genesis holders get auto-WL for future collections?

Yes, Genesis holders will get lifetime free future drops including Gen 2, VX, etc.

Will The Royal Phoenix have a token?

Yes, we will have an utility token but we want to create an ecosystem where our token has real utility and releasing a token too early can badly hurt our project.

Is this a DAO?

No. This is a Community driven project with a Multi-Signature Community Wallet controlled by Genesis holders aka "The Council". Council members will need to get to an agreement before using the available funds, but the project Founder signature will always be required (alongside with some 4-5 extra signatures).

Will the team be doxxed?

Yes, founders will dox before mint day. In the meantime you can follow the founder's twitter account @ItsStJimmy.
