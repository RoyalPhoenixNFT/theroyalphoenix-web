FAQs

What is The Royal Phoenix collection size?

There will be only 300 Generation 1 Royal Phoenix.

How many attributes will be in the collection?

Generation 1 will be automatically generated using 100+ different hand drawn attributes that will include: backgrounds, skins, hats, accesories, eyes, mouth, clothes and more.

What will be the mint price?

TBD - Expect a low mint price. Approx .05-.07ETH.

What are the mint dates?

TBD - This will be determined by the community.

What are the utilities of The Royal Phoenix?

TBD - This will be determined by the community.

How many Royal Phoenix NFTs can I mint?

Only 1 per wallet.

Do Gen-1 holders get auto-WL for future collections?

Yes, Gen-1 holders will get lifetime free future drops including Gen 2, VX, etc.

Will The Royal Phoenix have a token?

Yes, we will have an utility token but we want to create an ecosystem where our token has real utility and releasing a token too early can badly hurt our project.

Is this a DAO?

No. This is a Community driven project with a Multi-Signature Community Wallet controlled by Gen 1 holders aka "The Council". Council members will need to get to an agreement before using the available funds, but the project Founder signature will always be required (alongside with some 4-5 extra signatures).

Will the team be doxxed?

Yes, founders will dox before mint day. In the meantime you can follow the founder's twitter account @ItsStJimmy.
