FAQs

How can I mint an phoenix NFT?

Mint for OG members will begin at 6pm UTC on the 29th of April. Mint for WL members will begin at 7pm UTC and Public will open at 10pm UTC.

What is the total supply?

There will only ever be 4444 phoenix cyborgs

What is the cost to mint an phoenix NFT?

The price for OG and WL members will be 1.5 SOL. The price for public mint will be 1.75 SOL

How many phoenix NFTs can I mint?

OG members will have the opportunity to mint 2 NFTs during the OG mint phase. WL members will have the opportunity to mint 1 NFT during the WL mint phase. If the mint gets to public, then there is no limit on the amount of NFTs you can mint.

Which wallets will I be able to use to mint?

All compatible wallets with SOLANA are accepted, but we recommend Phantom wallet due to the friendly UI and ease of access.

How do I know if my phoenix is any rare?

We will be listed on Howrare and Solrarity which will allow you to determine the rank of your phoenix NFT
